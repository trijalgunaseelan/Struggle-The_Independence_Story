Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was born on 2 October 1869 in Porbandar, Kathiawar Agency, India. He got married at the age of 13 with Kasturbai Makhanji. One of the major roles in the act of freedom fight was played by Gandhiji. One of the Indian freedom fighters, Gandhiji was known for his non-violence protest. In his lifetime, he never fails to give importance to human rights. One of the leading figures of freedom movements is Mahatma Gandhi who is a true inspiration of all generations. Few crucial movements of his are Champaran Satyagraha, Kheda Satyagraha, Khilafat Movement, Non-Cooperation Movement, Civil-Disobedience Movement and Quit India Movement. He opposed few activities such as purdah, child marriage, dowry, sati. He urged the women to fight for their own self-development and he wished the emancipation of women.
