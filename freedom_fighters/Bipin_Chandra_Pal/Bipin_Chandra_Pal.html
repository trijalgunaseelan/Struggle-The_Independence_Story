Bipin Chandra Pal

Bipin Chandra Pal was born on 7 November 1858 Poil, Sylhet District, Bengal, India. In 1887, he joined the Congress party. At that time, he delivered a forceful speech in which he strictly pleaded that the Arms Act should be repeated in Chennai. He also participated in many sessions of the Congress and gave a great contribution to the nation's conciseness growth in India. He also encouraged the abandonment of foreign goods. Many of the Indian freedom fighters United and formed an association. He is also the one who invited in ours. They all together shot many revolutionary activities for India.
